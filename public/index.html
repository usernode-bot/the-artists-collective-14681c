The Artists' Collective

A community-owned art gallery.

New here? Two steps to join:

  1. Click Create your artist account → — registers you on-chain and opens the membership page.
  2. Return here and click Enter the studio → to access your dashboard.

Already a member? Just tap Enter the studio.

You're now a member-owner of The Artists' Collective.

  • You hold an ownership stake in the collective.
  • You have full voting rights on governance proposals — one member, one vote.
  • A share of every sale fee funds shared marketing, decided together.
